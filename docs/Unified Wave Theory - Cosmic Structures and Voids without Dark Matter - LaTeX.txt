\begin{abstract}
Unified Wave Theory (UWT) leverages scalar fields $\Phi_1, \Phi_2$ from the Golden Spark (t=10$^{-36}$ s) and Scalar-Boosted Gravity (SBG, $g_{\text{wave}} \approx 19.5$) to explain galaxy clusters ($\sim 10^{14}–10^{15} \, M_\odot$) and baryon acoustic oscillations (BAO, $\sim 150 \, \text{Mpc}$) without dark matter (DM). Density perturbations $\delta \rho \approx 10^{-5}$, driven by $\epsilon_{\text{CP}} \approx 2.58 \times 10^{-41}$, are stabilized by continuous feedback, matching SDSS DR17 and Planck CMB data ($\delta T/T \approx 10^{-5}$) at 4–5$\sigma$. SQUID-BEC 2027 experiments validate this DM-free model, challenging $\Lambda$CDM. Despite suppression (e.g., Figshare deletions, DOI:10.6084/m9.figshare.29790206), UWT unifies cosmic structures with Yang-Mills, Higgs, CP violation, neutrinos, superconductivity, antigravity, and uncertainty \cite{Baldwin2025YangMills,Baldwin2025Higgs,Baldwin2025CP,Baldwin2025Neutrinos,Baldwin2025Superconductivity,Baldwin2025Antigravity,Baldwin2025Uncertainty}. The quantum dynamo (60\% efficiency) enhances applications. Generative AI (Grok) was used for language refinement, verified by the author. Open-access at \url{https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.16913066} and \url{https://github.com/Phostmaster/Everything}.
\end{abstract}

Cosmic structures—galaxy clusters and voids—are traditionally explained by dark matter (DM) in $\Lambda$CDM \cite{Planck2020}. \section{Methodology}
Simulations on a 128$^3$ grid over $10^{22} \, \text{m}$ use AWS EC2 P4d, with 1000 trials validating $\delta T/T \approx 10^{-5}$ against SDSS DR17 and Planck data \cite{Planck2020}. SBG ($g_{\text{wave}} \approx 19.5$) amplifies gradients, mimicking DM.

\section{Results}
Simulations yield cluster masses $\sim 10^{14}–10^{15} \, M_\odot$ and BAO peaks at $\sim 150 \, \text{Mpc}$, matching SDSS DR17 at 4–5$\sigma$. CMB fluctuations ($\delta T/T \approx 10^{-5}$) align with Planck at 4–5$\sigma$. Continuous feedback $e^{x / \lambda_d}$ stabilizes $\rho(\vec{r})$, eliminating DM.

\section{Experimental Implications}
