This addendum to \emph{Antigravity via SQUID-BEC Field Manipulation} \cite{Baldwin2025Antigravity} addresses numerical instability in the simulation (\texttt{squid_bec_antigrav_760x_logistic.py}), achieving $\Delta m/m \approx -9 \times 10^{18}$ at 4--5$\sigma$. \subsection*{Stabilization via Pulsing Damping}
Instabilities from rapid field growth ($|\Phi_1| \to \infty$) were resolved using pulsing damping, $-0.0001 |\Phi_1 \Phi_2| \Phi_1 / (1 + |\Phi_1 \Phi_2|/10^4)$ when $|\Phi_1 \Phi_2| > 10^4$, with adaptive time steps $dt = 0.01 / (1 + |\Phi_1 \Phi_2|/10^4)$. Results:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Maximum: $|\Phi_1| \approx 1.07 \times 10^5$.
    \item Mean: $|\Phi_1 \Phi_2|^2 \approx 9.3 \times 10^{18}$.
    \item Mass perturbation: $\Delta m/m \approx -9 \times 10^{18}$.
    \item Energy density: $4.02 \times 10^{17} \, \text{J/m}^3$, $\eta = 10^9 \, \text{J/m}^3$.
\end{itemize}
